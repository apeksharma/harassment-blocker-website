About

Empathization refers to data-science products, based on user feedback and emphathetic innovation, aimed to mitigate online gender harassment: a sub-issue of gender inequality. The two-prong effort revolves around two types of users: people who send online gender harassment, and people who receive such harassment. The products are built upon humans who detected gender harassment in tweets, and then artificial intelligence (AI) algorithms that aimed to learn from that human detection. Currently, the product can reach 80% accuracy when it predicts online gender harassment, and has the capability to capture and either discourage or block over 1 million gender offensive tweets per week.
